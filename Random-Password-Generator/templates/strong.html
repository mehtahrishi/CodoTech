What Makes a Password Strong?

Creating a strong password is one of the most critical steps you can take to secure your online life. Here are the key ingredients:

  • Length: Longer is always better. Aim for at least 12-16 characters. Each additional character exponentially increases the password's complexity.
  • Complexity: Use a mix of all character types:
    • Uppercase letters (A-Z)
    • Lowercase letters (a-z)
    • Numbers (0-9)
    • Symbols (!@#$%^&*)
  • Unpredictability: Avoid using common words, phrases, or personal information like your name, birthday, or pet's name. "Password123" is not your friend!
  • Uniqueness: Use a different, unique password for every single one of your accounts. If one site is breached, your other accounts remain safe.

This app generates passwords that meet all these criteria, giving you a strong foundation for your security.
